Senior Backend Engineer (Python)

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to design, build, and scale
the services that power our platform. You will work closely with product
and infrastructure teams to ship reliable, well-tested APIs.

Responsibilities
- Design and implement scalable REST/GraphQL APIs using Python (FastAPI/Django/Flask)
- Own service reliability: monitoring, alerting, incident response
- Collaborate with frontend and data teams on API contracts
- Write clean, well-tested, well-documented code
- Mentor junior engineers and participate in code reviews
- Contribute to architecture decisions for microservices and data pipelines

Requirements
- 4+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and at least one modern web framework (FastAPI, Django, Flask)
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL/MySQL) and caching layers (Redis)
- Familiarity with containerization (Docker) and cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure)
- Solid understanding of CI/CD pipelines and automated testing
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Nice to Have
- Experience with LangChain, LangGraph, or LLM-powered applications
- Experience with Kubernetes and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform)
- Prior experience in a fast-paced startup environment
- Contributions to open-source projects

Education
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent practical experience
